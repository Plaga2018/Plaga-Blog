About Plaga

The game Plaga is set in the early 19th century world of Bay-la-zel, where a terrible blight which the townspeople have named Plaga. On your journey there will be many trials that will test not only your combat skills but also your intellect. Come now and slay these demons and send the Plaga back to wherever it came from.
