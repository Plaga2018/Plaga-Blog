About Plaga

The once prosperous town of Bailazel has come under many great horrors and distress. Villagers whisper of something called... the Plaga. Many a men had tried to send it back to the depths from which it came, but alas... all have failed.

Bailazel... doomed to sit in the darkness. Grasping to the heavens for the day a hero may purge this demonic blight from the land.
